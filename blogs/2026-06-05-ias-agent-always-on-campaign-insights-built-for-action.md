IAS Agent, launched in March 2026, automates campaign performance analysis within the Signal UI Overview Dashboard to deliver actionable insights in minutes rather than hours. The tool leverages 15 years of data analysis and 280 billion daily data interactions to provide recommendations with transparent reasoning, allowing marketers at any experience level to optimize campaigns faster. Users maintain full control, as they can customize or override any recommendation to align with specific campaign goals and brand priorities.
